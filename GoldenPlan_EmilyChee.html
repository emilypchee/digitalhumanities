GoldenPlan App

Purpose/Goal

This app serves to help people set up an event or meet up. It develops a preliminary agenda, selecting the right attendees, and deciding where and when to hold the meeting and confirm availability of the space. It reduces miscommunication and the risk of plans falling through. Students find this app helpful whenever they want to make study group plans or spontaneous trips to the city. With this app, all plans will be successful.

Design Problem

At school and at work, people often need to meet up and collaborate on work. Sometimes people are too busy and people don’t communicate. These meetings don’t follow through. GoldenPlan App efficiently finds the most compatible time and place for a meet up by cross checking between people’s calendars. Everyone’s information is kept private and users have freedom to decline or accept potential meeting ideas. The calendar feature in this app strengthens the social fabric because it connects people efficiently and keeps meetings running smoothly and on schedule. This resource allows people to get their job done in a timely fashion without running the risk of failed plans. It sends out notifications that will help remind users of their scheduled activities.
