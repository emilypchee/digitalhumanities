SCENARIO #2

Context

Brian is a first year Digital Humanities major. He has a group project deadline for his UCLA Introduction to Programming class this Friday. It’s already Tuesday. The professor suggest the time to complete the assignment is about three hours. He needs to meet up with his three groupmates to assign tasks, but he’s awfully busy. He works 30 hours a week. He has FAST club meetings every Wednesday at 7PM. There are other homework assignments he has to complete as well. The group is cutting it too close to the deadline and he needs to come up with a plan ASAP.

Use Case

Luckily, his roommate introduced him to the GoldenPlan app. Brian adds his personal schedule to the calendar feature. He then adds his group mates as friends through their phone number. He creates a group chat and highlights the Friday deadline on their group chat calendar. Before dinner that night, his phone gently vibrates to indicate GoldenPlan had send his a notification. GoldenPlan alerts him all the potential one hour slot times he can meet up with his group mates. All he has to do is select “Going!” or “Can’t make it.” Following dinner, Brian gets another notification indicating that on his calendar, he now has a meeting time with his group mates tomorrow after his shift at 5PM at Rieber Hall LLC Lounge. The location of the meet up has been decided through the app based on everyone's location. Without much confrontation, Brian gets to finish his project on time without any complications.
