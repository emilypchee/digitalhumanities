SCENARIO #1

Context

Victoria lives in UCLA Hedrick Hall with 60 floormates. There’s a mix of first and second year students. There’s a three day weekend coming up and she wants to hike up the Hollywood Sign with her floormates because she loves nature and exercising. She has a hard time leading a large group and is unsure how to get a group to hike without having them bail on her last minute. She doesn’t have work for once and has an additional day to do homework. Victoria thinks this is the perfect way to meet new people and connect with the people on her floor. Maybe she’ll find friends that have the same class as her or share similar hobbies. Plus, the hike will allow her to take more pictures and post them on her social media. It will give her more likes and follows. The more people come, the less she’ll have to pay for transportation because there will be split bill.

Use Case

To get people to come with her, she uses the GoldenPlan app. Victoria creates a floor group chat and highlights the Monday hike trip on the group chat calendar. Everyone receives a notification of the event. Students must respond “Going!” or “Can’t make it.” before a certain time or they will automatically get kicked out of the group chat. The app continues to send out notifications accordingly. It’ll send out notifications about departure time, location to meet up, transportation types with estimated prices, where to eat, and what time they need to return back to campus. If students do not respond before the app selective deadline, then they will get kicked out of the group chat, making the planning group smaller and smaller. A week before the actual event, GoldenPlan will create an official group chat in which members can text and chat about the minute additional details. They can add more alerts onto the calendar feature. GoldenPlan app efficiently brings together a group of students on the floor that want to go hiking on their three day weekend. It weeds out those who are not interested and helps develop an organized schedule for those who are going. Victoria now has a group to hike and network with and she did not have to go through a stressful time bringing everyone together.
